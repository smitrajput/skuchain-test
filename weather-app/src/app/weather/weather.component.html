Legend

  1. Day: day 1 represents tomorrow, day 2 is day after tomorrow, etc.
  2. Summary: can be one of 'clear-day', 'clear-night', 'rain', 'snow', 'sleet', 'wind', 'fog', 'cloudy', 'partly-cloudy-day', or 'partly-cloudy-night'
  3. Humidity: The relative humidity, between 0 and 1, inclusive.
  4. Wind Speed: The wind speed in miles per hour.
  5. Cloud Cover: The percentage of sky occluded by clouds, between 0 and 1, inclusive.
  6. Precip Accumulation: The amount of snowfall accumulation expected to occur (over the hour or day, respectively), in inches. (If no snowfall is expected, this property will not be defined.)
  7. Precip Intensity: The intensity (in inches of liquid water per hour) of precipitation occurring at the given time. This value is conditional on probability (that is, assuming any precipitation occurs at all).
  8. Precip Probability: The probability of precipitation occurring, between 0 and 1, inclusive.
  9. Apparent Temp High: The daytime high apparent(feels like) temperature.
  10. Apparent Temp Low: The overnight low apparent(feels like) temperature.
